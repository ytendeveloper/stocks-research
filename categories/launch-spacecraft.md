## Theme Overview

Launch and spacecraft companies sit at the foundation of the space economy — nothing happens in orbit or beyond without them. With launch costs plummeting and government/commercial demand surging, this category captures the picks-and-shovels opportunity of space commercialization. Lunar programs (Artemis, CLPS) add a second growth vector beyond LEO constellation buildouts.
