## Theme Overview

Aerospace and defense supply chains depend on a narrow set of specialty materials — titanium, carbon fiber, rare earths, and superalloys — where supply is constrained and geopolitically sensitive. The dual tailwinds of commercial space expansion and global rearmament are tightening supply for these critical inputs. Companies with domestic production capacity for strategic materials hold pricing power and long-term contract visibility.
