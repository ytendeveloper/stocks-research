## Theme Overview

The AI infrastructure buildout requires massive investment in networking, optics, and compute — creating a multi-year capex supercycle. Optical interconnects are the bottleneck as AI clusters scale beyond single racks, making companies like Lumentum, Coherent, and AAOI critical enablers. The full stack from GPU compute (NVIDIA) through networking silicon (Broadcom, Marvell) to switches (Arista) represents a deeply interconnected growth opportunity.
