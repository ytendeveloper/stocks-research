## Theme Overview

Modern warfare is shifting decisively toward autonomous and unmanned systems, as demonstrated by conflicts in Ukraine and the broader global rearmament cycle. Small tactical drones, loitering munitions, and AI-enabled autonomy are reshaping defense procurement away from legacy platforms. This category spans the full spectrum from speculative small-cap drone plays to established defense primes adapting to the new paradigm.
