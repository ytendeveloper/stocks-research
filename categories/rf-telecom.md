## Theme Overview

RF and telecom infrastructure underpins both terrestrial 5G rollouts and the explosive growth of satellite communications. As constellations like Starlink and Kuiper scale to tens of thousands of satellites, demand for RF components, signal processing, and ground-station equipment is accelerating. AI-driven radio access networks (AI-RAN) represent the next evolution in how telecom networks are built and optimized.
