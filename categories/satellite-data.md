## Theme Overview

Satellite data is becoming essential infrastructure for defense, climate monitoring, agriculture, and global connectivity. The shift from bespoke government satellites to mass-produced commercial constellations is unlocking new markets and recurring revenue streams. Direct-to-device connectivity (ASTS, Kuiper) represents a potential paradigm shift in how billions of people access the internet.
