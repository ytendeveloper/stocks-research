## KEY FINDINGS & THEMES

### The Top 6 Multi-Bagger Candidates (in order of conviction):

**1. LUNR ($5.5B) — Lunar Economy Monopoly**
- Revenue tripling YoY, first positive adj. EBITDA ($2.7M in Q1)
- $1.1B backlog, near-monopoly on NASA lunar delivery
- Acquired Lanteris + Goonhilly for vertical integration
- Risk: NASA budget dependency, lunar mission failure risk

**2. KTOS ($10.5B) — Autonomous Combat Aircraft Leader**
- Won CCA Valkyrie contract (USMC), $231.5M
- $156B defense reconciliation bill allocates funds for Kratos programs
- Stock pulled back 58% from highs — attractive entry
- Risk: Production ramp execution, government budget cycles

**3. STM ($55B) — The Hidden Starlink Play**
- 90% market share in LEO satellite semiconductors
- Shipped 7.5B chips to Starlink, targeting 10B by 2027
- $3B cumulative space semiconductor revenue target 2026-2028
- LEO revenue grew from $175M (2021) to ~$1B (2026)
- Risk: Overall margin compression from auto/industrial weakness

**4. BKSY ($1.3B) — Defense ISR At Bargain Valuation**
- $1.3B market cap with $345M backlog and 30%+ growth
- Gen-3 constellation upgrade, $30M international defense wins
- Approaching EBITDA profitability in 2026
- Risk: Small revenue base, defense contract concentration

**5. ONDS ($4.6B) — Explosive Drone Border Defense**
- 1,065% Q1 revenue growth, $390M+ FY26 guide
- Prime contractor for autonomous border protection (thousands of drones)
- Counter-UAS at European airports
- Risk: Sustainability of 10x growth, profitability unclear

**6. AAOI ($15B) — AI Optics With Highest Torque**
- Revenue tripling ($455M→$1.1B+), profitability inflection imminent
- Smallest cap among AI optics peers ($15B vs $75B LITE/COHR)
- Vertical integration with in-house laser production
- Risk: Customer concentration, 441% YTD run already happened

### Notable "Fallen Angel" Opportunities:
- **AVAV** ($169, down 60% from $418): Military drone leader with $3.5B backlog, messy BlueHalo integration creating entry point
- **KTOS** ($56, down 58% from $134): CCA winner, selling off on short-term sentiment
- **RKLB** ($131): Best space company but $76B cap limits upside — watch for pullbacks

### Surprising Finds:
- **STM's Starlink dominance** — 90% LEO chip share is massively underappreciated
- **CRS (Carpenter)** — NASA GRX-810 rocket engine alloy is a genuine space differentiator
- **NOK on the Moon** — Nokia deployed 4G on the lunar surface via IM-2; NTN roadmap for satellite-5G

### Stocks to Avoid for Multi-Bagger Thesis:
- Mega-caps (NVDA, AVGO, AMZN, RTX, LMT) — great companies but math doesn't work at these sizes
- Commodity plays (AA, FCX, TECK) — copper/aluminum are space-adjacent but not differentiated
- Slow growers (IRDM at 5%, YSS at 9%, PKE flat) — need acceleration to multiply
